# Asset Checklist

Use this as a staging list for the files that will make the portfolio feel more personal and less template-like.

## Identity

- Favicon as SVG and PNG. Suggested names: `public/favicon.svg` and `public/favicon.png`.
- Logo or wordmark if you want one beyond the current `LM` mark. Suggested location: `public/images/brand/`.
- Optional social preview image for link sharing. Suggested size: 1200 by 630 pixels.

## Profile

- Current headshot, high resolution original.
- Cropped square headshot for compact cards and hero panels.
- Optional casual/profile-adjacent photo if you want the site to feel less formal.

Suggested location: `public/images/profile/`.

## Resume

- Current resume PDF. Suggested path: `public/resume/resume.pdf`.
- Current HTML resume export. Suggested path: `public/resume/resume.html`.
- Last updated date to store in `src/config/site.ts`.

## Projects

For each project you want public, gather:

- 1 hero image that explains the project quickly.
- 2 to 5 supporting images, screenshots, diagrams, build photos, or result photos.
- A short caption for each image.
- Any public links: GitHub, demo, paper, poster, video, or external article.

Suggested location pattern: `public/images/projects/project-slug/`.

## Background And Personal Flavor

- University of Michigan campus photos you are allowed to use.
- Lab, workshop, desk, robotics, or project-process photos.
- Club or organization photos/logos if they are okay to publish.
- A few non-school personal images only if they support the story you want the site to tell.

Avoid adding large raw photo dumps directly to the repo. Pick intentional images, resize/compress them, and keep originals somewhere else.
